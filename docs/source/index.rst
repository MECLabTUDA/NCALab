Welcome to NCALab's documentation!
======================================

NCALab is a framework designed to streamline the creation and analysis of Neural Cellular Automata (NCA) implementations.
With NCALab, users can effortlessly explore various applications, including image segmentation, classification, and synthesis.
Designed for researchers, developers, and enthusiasts alike, NCALab enables users to explore a variety of
downstream tasks, including image segmentation, classification, and synthesis.

This documentation serves as a comprehensive guide to understanding and utilizing NCALab's features.
Here, you'll find detailed instructions, examples, and best practices to help you get started.
